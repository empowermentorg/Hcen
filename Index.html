About HCEN

HCEN is a grassroots safety and support network for sex workers and marginalized individuals in Humboldt County, California. Our mission is to reduce harm, provide emergency tools, and build trust within our community.

What We Offer

  • 📍 A “Bad Date” Database for real-time alerts
  • 🚨 Emergency SOS button for trusted contact notification
  • 🧠 Referrals to local services (housing, counseling, legal aid)
  • 🔒 Community-driven safety ratings and shared experiences

Our Vision

This project was built in memory of a close friend who was part of the industry and is no longer with us. Her loss reminds us why this work matters. As a Native-led initiative, HCEN also reflects the strength and survival of communities that have long been overlooked.
