About HCEN

The Humboldt County Empowerment Network (HCEN) is a grassroots safety and resource hub built in memory of loved ones who faced violence, addiction, and systemic neglect. This project was created out of friendship, survival, and a commitment to reducing harm. HCEN offers tools and support for people in high-risk situations like sex work, domestic violence, homelessness, and drug use.

What We Offer

  • 📍 Bad Date Database – View or Submit Reports
  • 🚨 Emergency SOS Button – Try the SOS Tool
  • 🧠 Referral Network – Browse Local Resources
  • 🔐 Safety Reviews – Contribute or Read Safety Info
  • 💡 Tech & Wellness Toolkit – See Apps & Tools

Our Vision

HCEN was created in memory of people who deserved better—people who were loved and should still be here. We believe in tools that empower people to stay safe, get help, and feel less alone. This work is grounded in real experiences, not institutions. It’s about practical compassion and community.
