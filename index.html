About HCEN

The Humboldt County Empowerment Network (HCEN) is a grassroots support and safety project built from lived experience and love. It began in Humboldt, shaped by the realities of survival, violence, addiction, and healing.

HCEN is not an institution — it's a set of tools made by someone who lived through it, for others still walking the same path. It exists to make people feel less alone and more prepared.

Built From Love

This project is dedicated to someone who meant everything to me — someone I lost too soon. She had fire and honesty and strength in a world that tried to erase her. This is for her, and for the people like her still here.

I won’t use her name. Her family deserves peace, and her story is sacred. What I can do is build something that keeps others safe — something that honors the survival, the love, and the pain that created it.

Tools for Safety & Survival

  • 📍 Bad Date Database – A community-powered alert system to warn about dangerous people or situations.
  • 🚨 SOS Button – An emergency tool that sends your location to someone you trust.
  • 🧠 Referral Network – A directory of local resources for housing, health, legal aid, and more.
  • 🔐 Safety Reviews – Anonymous reviews that help people avoid harm and share trusted places or people.
  • 💡 Tech & Wellness Toolkit – Curated apps and tools for staying safe, sober, and connected.
